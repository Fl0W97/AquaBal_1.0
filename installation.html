Installation Instructions

1. Place the AquaBal in your water tank, ensuring it is fully submerged.

2. The AquaBal will begin working immediately to maintain aerobic conditions and prevent odor formation.

3. For optimal performance, replace the AquaBal every 6-12 months, depending on water quality and usage.

4 . If you notice any issues with water quality or odor, check the AquaBal for damage and replace it if necessary.

Correct cable usage

Make sure that the cable of the AquaBal is not damaged and that the device is fully submerged in the water. The AquaBal should be placed in a location where it can effectively circulate the water, such as near the center of the tank or close to the water inlet. IF the cable is damaged, please contact our customer support for a replacement. Do not attempt to use the AquaBal if the cable is damaged, as this may pose a safety risk.

Tank Size Considerations

One AquaBal is sufficient for tanks up to 1000 liters. For larger tanks, multiple AquaBal devices can be used to ensure optimal performance and water quality.
